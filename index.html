What is the Zalympix

The Games of
Great Cannabis.

Founded in 2021 by a close-knit crew of growers and heads, the Zalympix exists to shine a light on the people who deserve it — the legacy cultivators, the boutique breeders, the small-batch obsessives building the best cannabis on Earth.

What began as an invite-only night for California's most respected flower has become a national institution — a competition where entries are stripped of their branding and judged blind, so the only thing that wins is the weed itself.

Judged Blind

Every entry is anonymized — no logos, no packaging, no politics. Just the flower on its merits.

The People Decide

Curated judge kits go to real consumers who vote, alongside a hand-picked industry panel.

The Long Game

120+ day cultivation windows let finalists flower fresh batches — quality over a rushed calendar.

How A Champion Is Made

The Road to the Podium

One California season, five deliberate stages. No shortcuts, no branded bias — the format is built to reward the grower, not the marketing budget.

November 2025

The Preliminary Round

Any licensed cultivator can enter. For the 2026 California season, 108 entries arrived from farms across the state — the widest, most open field the Zalympix has ever fielded.

Open to all licensed brands

By December 29, 2025

The Judges Cut

Entries are stripped of all branding and handed to 28 hand-selected industry insiders — growers, breeders and connoisseurs who narrow the field to the finalists on flavor, structure and nose alone.

Blind · anonymized
03
120+ Day Window

Fresh Cultivation

Finalists get a full flowering window to grow and harvest a fresh, dedicated batch for judging — no jarred-up leftovers. This is the Zalympix's signature "long game."

Freshly grown for the finals
